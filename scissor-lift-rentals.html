Floor conditions & model choice

Choose scissor lifts for the way your site is built

The right scissor lift depends not just on height but also on how it travels from the unloading point to each working zone.

Slab scissor lifts

  • Best for smooth, level floors and marked travel paths.
  • Compact footprint for moving through doors and corridors.
  • Non-marking tyres to protect finished surfaces and tiles.

Rough-terrain scissor lifts

  • Higher ground clearance for unfinished slabs and outdoor zones.
  • Wider decks for more tools and two or more technicians.
  • Useful where conditions change as construction progresses.
