About us

A boom lift–focused access partner

Rental pattern

Simple ways to plan your boom lift rentals

Use these bands as a guide. Actual pricing depends on model, duration, location and support needed.

Project-based
Ideal for quick jobs and limited-scope activities.
  • Suitable for maintenance or small upgrade works.
  • Rapid deployment where models are available.
  • Flexible extension based on progress.
Planned
For structured activities and multiple work fronts.
  • Coordinated deployment for several booms.
  • Planning support before the shutdown window.
  • Suitable for industrial and infra projects.
Ongoing
When access at height is a consistent requirement.
  • Helps avoid repeated mobilisation for the same site.
  • Useful for large campuses or long upgrade programmes.
  • Discuss duration and expected utilisation upfront.
